About the Pacific Ocean Playground

In the Pacific Northwest, natural beauty and the largest ocean in the world surround us. Pacific Ocean Playground is an educational game platform for young teenagers to learn about the Pacific Ocean and test their knowledge. Teens can track their improvement through metrics on their profile and compare their scores to their friends. Pacific Ocean Playground is a fun tool to learn about our ocean!
